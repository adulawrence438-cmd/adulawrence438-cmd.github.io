CAD Design

Wall-Mounted Cable / Probe Holder

Overview: Designed and modeled a wall-mounted cable and probe holder using SolidWorks to organize and support hanging cables in a general engineering environment.

Design Features

  • 40 × 60 × 5 mm backplate with countersunk mounting holes.
  • Reinforced L-shaped hook with 40 mm projection.
  • 5 mm fillet at the hook–backplate junction to reduce stress.
  • Rounded edges to prevent cable damage.

Material & Manufacturing

  • Designed for PETG 3D printing due to its strength.
  • Optimized geometry for repeated loading.

Centrifuge Tube Adapter

Overview: Designed and modeled a centrifuge tube adapter in SolidWorks to allow secure placement of smaller test tubes inside a larger centrifuge bucket.

Design Features

  • Outer cylindrical body sized for accurate bucket fitting.
  • Top retaining lip to prevent slipping.
  • Internal bore sized to hold standard 5 mL centrifuge tubes.

Engineering Considerations

  • Clearance fit applied to balance stability and ease of use.
