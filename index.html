Portable Medical Suction Machine (Aspirator)

Overview: Designed a compact, high-performance portable medical suction unit for clinical and emergency use. The design focuses on ergonomic handling, effective fluid collection, and pump protection through an integrated overflow safety valve.

Design Dimensions

  • Housing Size: 380mm x 170mm x 285mm
  • Collection Jar: 1000mL Graduated Polysulfone (PSU)
  • Tubing: Ø12mm x 7mm Medical-grade Silicone
  • Inlet Connector: 6mm Conical tapered fit

Engineering Notes

  • Overflow Protection: Integrated a mechanical float-valve system to prevent fluid ingress into the vacuum pump.
  • Control Interface: Modeled an analog vacuum gauge (-75kPa range) and a precision rotary regulator for airflow control.
  • Filtration: Dedicated chamber for single-use bacterial filters to ensure aseptic operation.
  • Aesthetics: Contoured handle design for rapid transport in emergency wards.
