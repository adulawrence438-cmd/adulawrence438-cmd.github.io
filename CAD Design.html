Wall-Mounted Cable / Probe Holder

Overview: Designed and modeled a wall-mounted cable and probe holder using SolidWorks to organize and support hanging cables in a general engineering environment. The design focuses on load-bearing performance, durability, and safe cable handling.

Design Features

  • 40 × 60 × 5 mm backplate with countersunk mounting holes for flush installation.
  • Reinforced L-shaped hook with 40 mm projection and 15 mm retaining lip.
  • 5 mm fillet at the hook–backplate junction to reduce stress concentration.
  • Rounded edges to prevent cable damage and snagging.

Material & Manufacturing

  • Designed for PETG 3D printing due to its strength and impact resistance.
  • Optimized geometry for repeated loading and long-term use.

Centrifuge Tube Adapter

Overview: Designed and modeled a centrifuge tube adapter in SolidWorks to allow secure placement of smaller test tubes inside a larger centrifuge bucket. The design emphasizes precise clearance fitting to ensure stability during operation while allowing easy insertion and removal.

Design Features

  • Outer cylindrical body sized for accurate bucket fitting.
  • Top retaining lip to prevent the adapter from slipping into the bucket.
  • Internal bore sized to hold standard 5 mL centrifuge tubes.
  • Rounded internal base to support the tube and reduce stress.

Engineering Considerations

  • Clearance fit applied to balance stability and ease of use.
  • Rounded geometry to reduce vibration and stress concentration.
  • Designed for PETG 3D printing to ensure durability and chemical resistance.
