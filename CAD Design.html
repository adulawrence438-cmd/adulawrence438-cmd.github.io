Wall-Mounted Cable / Probe Holder (CAD & Reverse Engineering)

Overview: Designed and modeled a wall-mounted cable and probe holder using SolidWorks to support and organize hanging cables. The component was engineered with a reinforced load-bearing hook and a compact backplate featuring countersunk mounting holes for flush wall installation.

Design Details

  • Backplate dimensions: 40 × 60 × 5 mm for stable wall mounting.
  • L-shaped hook with 40 mm projection and 15 mm retaining lip.
  • 5 mm fillet at the hook–plate junction to reduce stress concentration.
  • Rounded edges to prevent cable damage and snagging.

Material & Manufacturing

  • Designed for PETG 3D printing due to its strength, impact resistance, and durability.
  • Optimized geometry to withstand repeated loading without failure.
