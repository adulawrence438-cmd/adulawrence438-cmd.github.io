BME Clinical Training Challenges in Ghana

Academic Research | October 2025

Research Topic: EXAMINING THE TECHNICAL CHALLENGES BIOMEDICAL ENGINEERING STUDENTS ENCOUNTER DURING CLINICAL TRAINING AND THEIR IMPACT ON PROFESSIONAL DEVELOPMENT IN GHANA.

120 Students Surveyed
55% Equipment Unavailable
62% Operation Struggles
60% Reduced Confidence

Abstract Preview

This study explores the gap between theory and practice in BME education across four Ghanaian universities. Findings indicate that technical difficulties and limited hands-on practice significantly impact readiness for professional practice. The study underscores the urgent need for structured mentorship and improved hospital-university partnerships.
